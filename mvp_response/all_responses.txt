Question 4: What are the opinions of UX researchers/designers regarding the use of AI in user research?
Relevant quotes across interviews:
 - Interview 1 (Analyzing UX Research Workflows and Challenges): "AI does its best, but it doesn't have the human touch."
Response: The opinion on the use of AI in user research, as expressed in the available interviews, is cautious. While there is an acknowledgment of AI's efficiency in automating tasks like transcription and highlighting potential areas of interest, there is also a preference for human analysis. This preference stems from a mistrust in AI's ability to fully capture and interpret nuanced insights, indicating a belief in the irreplaceable value of the human touch in analyzing user interviews.
